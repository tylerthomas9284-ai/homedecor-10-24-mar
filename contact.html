Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I schedule a consultation?

We typically have availability within 3-5 business days. For urgent requests, please call us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve New York City and surrounding areas. We also offer virtual consultations for clients outside our primary service area.

What should I prepare for my first consultation?

Please have photos of your space, room measurements, and a general budget range in mind. We'll guide you through the rest during our consultation.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes! We offer comprehensive virtual design consultations for clients who prefer remote service or are located outside our primary service area.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and checks. Payment terms will be outlined in your service agreement.
